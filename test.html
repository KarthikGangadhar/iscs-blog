Dr. Sungyong Jung

Associate Professor

Dr. Sungyong Jung received a Ph.D degree in electrical engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology in 2002. His doctoral research focused on CMOS optical transceiver circuit design including circuit parasitic effects and analysis. Upon graduation, he was involved in optical transceiver design to improve spectral efficiency with Quellan Inc as an advanced circuit engineer. Currently, he is an associate chair and associate professor in electrical engineering department at University of Texas at Arlington. He has established integrated sensing circuits and systems (ISCS) laboratory, which specializes in integrated circuits and systems design for chemical and bio sensing application and for wireless communication systems. His research interests include IC design for wireless communications and radar applications, bio/chemical sensing circuits and systems, hearing aid device, wireless charging systems, and high-speed CMOS analog and mixed-signal circuit design.
